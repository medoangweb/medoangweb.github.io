+/ The French phonetic alphabet/ IPA chart for French

The French phonetic alphabet, also called the French spelling alphabet consists of 39 sounds in total. In the French IPA chart, there are 12 oral vowels, 4 nasal vowels, 3 semi-vowels or semi-consonants, 20 consonants, all of which are illustrated in the chart below.
