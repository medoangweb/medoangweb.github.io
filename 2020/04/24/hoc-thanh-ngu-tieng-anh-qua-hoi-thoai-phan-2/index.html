Học thành ngữ tiếng Anh qua hội thoại (phần 2)

Sử dụng thành ngữ là một cách để nói hay, chuẩn như người bản ngữ. Các bạn đã nắm vững các thành ngữ trong phần 1 của series học thành ngữ chưa? Các bạn có thể xem lại các thành ngữ đó tại đây. Trong bài viết này, chúng ta sẽ học tiếp những thành ngữ mới trong đoạn hội thoại giữa 2 vợ chồng Bob và Susan nhé.

BOB RETURNS HOME WITH BAD NEWS

Bob tells his wife Susan that he lost his job. Susan suggests that he start his own business. 

Susan: What’s the matter,dear? 

Bob: Susan, I got canned today at work. 

Susan: But Bob, you were Peter’s right-hand man! 

Bob: Yes, and he stabbed me in the back. 

 Susan: Keep your chin up. Maybe he’ll change his mind and take you back. 

Bob: When pigs fly! Once he makes up his mind, he never changes it. Besides, I told him off. 

Susan: Look on the bright side: you won’t have to set eyes on Peter ever again. 

Bob: Thank goodness for that! 

Susan: Hang in there. I’m sure you won’t be out of work for long. 

Bob: In the mean time, we’ll have to live from hand to mouth. 

Susan: Don’t get too stressed out,Bob.  We’ll make ends meet. 

Bob: I can always get a job at McDonald’s as a last resort. 

Susan: I don’t think they’re hiring right now. 

Bob: If worse comes to worst, we can sell our home and move into a tent. 

Susan:Let’s think big! Maybe you can start your own business. 

Bob: Easier said than done! 

                  §§§§§§

Các bạn đã thấy bao nhiêu thành ngữ trong đoạn hội thoai trên rồi? Hãy cùng xem những thành ngữ này nhé:

  1. (to) change one’s mind: to change one’s opinion or decision 

🍀Brandon wasn’t going to take a vacation this year, but then he changed his mind and went to Bora for two weeks. 

=> change one’s mind: thay đổi ý định

2. easier said than done: more difficult than you think 

🍀Moving into a new home is easier said than done. 

=> easier said than done: nói dễ hơn làm

3. (to) get canned (slang): to lose one’s job; to get fired 

🍀Lisa is a lousy secretary. She deserves to get canned! 

=> (to) get canned: bị sa thải

SYNONYMS: to get sacked; to be given the ax 

4. (to) hang in there: to persevere; to not give up 

🍀Hang in there, Don! Your invention will soon be a success. 

=> (to) hang in there: hãy kiên trì, đừng bỏ cuộc

5. if worse comes to worst: in the worst case; if absolutely necessary 

🍀Ted’s car isn’t running well. If worse comes to worst, he can take the bus to school. 

=> if worse comes to worst: trong trường hợp xấu nhất

6. (to) keep one’s chin up: to stay positive 

🍀Keep your chin up! You’ll find your lost dog soon. 

=> (to) keep one’s chin up: Hãy suy nghĩ tích cực.

7. last resort: if there are no other alternatives left; the last  solution for getting out of a difficulty 

🍀I don’t like taking medicine. I’ll only take it as a last resort. 

=> last resort: phương án cuối cùng

8. (to) live from hand to mouth: to barely have enough money tosurvive 

🍀George is really poor. He lives from hand to mouth. 

=> (to) live from hand to mouth: nghèo

9. (to) look on the bright side: to be optimistic; to think about the positive part or aspect of a situation 

🍀You lost your job? Look on the bright side, now you’ll have more free time! 

=> (to) look on the bright side: Hãy lạc quan lên.

10. (to) make ends meet: to manage one’s money so as to have enough to live on; to be okay financially 

🍀If you can’t make ends meet, you’ll need to start spending less. 

=> (to) make ends meet: trang trải cuộc sống

11. out of work: unemployed; not working 

🍀Bob is out of work.Do you know any body who might want to hire him? 

=> out of work: thất nghiệp

12. right-hand man: the most helpful assistant or employee 

🍀Juan’s right-hand man helps him make all of his decisions. 

=> right-hand man: cánh tay phải, trợ thủ đắc lực

13. (to) set eyes on: to look at; to see for the first time 

🍀Ted was in love from the moment he set eyes on Amber. 

=> (to) set eyes on: gặp lần đầu tiên

14. (to) stab someone in the back: to betray someone 

🍀You’re firing me after all I’ve done for this company? You’re really stabbing me in the back! 

=> (to) stab someone in the back: phản bội ai đó

15. (to be) stressed out: under severe strain; very anxious 

🍀Alis so stressed out about his job that he can’t sleep at night. 

=> (to be) stressed out: rất lo lắng

16. (to) tell off: to scold; to tell some one in strong words what one really thinks 

🍀When Ted showed up for chemistry class a half an hour late, his teacher really told him off.

=> (to) tell off: mắng ai đó một cách giận dữ

17. Thank goodness: I’m grateful; I’m relieved 

🍀When Ted came home at 4a.m. last Sunday, his mother said, “Thank goodness you’re home I was so worried about you.” 

=> Thank goodness: Cảm ơn Chúa

18. (to) think big: to set high goals 

🍀Why run for Governor of NewYork? Think big: run for President of the United States! 

=> (to) think big: đặt mục tiêu cao

19. What’s the matter?: What’s the problem? 

🍀What’s the matter, Bob? You don’t look very happy. 

=> What’s the matter?: Có chuyện gì vậy?

20. When pigs fly!(slang): never 

🍀Will Ted teach Nicole how to play the guitar? When pigs fly! 

=> When pigs fly!: Không đời nào!

SYNONYMS: when hell freezes over; never in a million years 

🐾Để nhớ các thành ngữ và từ lóng trên lâu hơn, chúng ta hãy làm bài tập nhỏ này nhé:

Fill in the blank with the missing word.

1)What’s the …………….? You look upset. 

a)situation          b)issue             c)matter

2)I know I can trust you. You would never stab me in the ………

a)leg                     b)back             c)arm

3)If Bob and Susan run out of money, they can always borrow  money from Susan’s sister as a ………….. resort. 

a)final                 b)first                c)last

4)You look really stressed ……………. Why don’t you sit down, 

relax, and have a cup of tea? 

a)about              b)in                   c)out 

5)Bob, everything will be fine. You just need to keep your chin ……….. and remember that tomorrow is another day. 

a)up                     b)down            c)above 

6)I just can’t make up my ………… whether to order chicken or fish. 

a)head                b)brain             c)mind 

7)Nicole accidentally stepped on Ted’s guitar. Ted got really 

angry and told her …………..

a)off                   b)out                   c)away 

8)Your husband left you for his psychologist? Hang ……….. there! I’m sure he’ll realize she’s crazy and then come back to you. 

a)up                   b)in                     c)out

Các bạn có thể viết câu trả lời ở phần bình luận bên dưới để mình sửa bài. Chúc vốn thành ngữ tiếng Anh của các bạn ngày càng nhiều!💝
