Learn English with the application Gorgeously – Good news app

There are many ways to learn English such as doing grammar exercises, reading books, watching movies with subtitles, reading newspapers, etc. In this article, I am going to share an effective way to learn foreign languages ​​with the application Gorgeously – Good news app on smartphone.

Content:

 🍀Gorgeously app – Good news app

 🍀Benefits of learning English by reading news

 🍀Steps to learn English by reading news

Let’s start right away!

🍀Gorgeously app – Good news app

+/ This is an application that synthesizes only the good news in all fields (science, technology, sports, culture, economy, education …), which makes you always think positively and focus on your goal without being distracted by bad news.

+/ Readers can like and comment below each article to practice writing skills, critical thinking skills, and express personal opinions. This is extremely useful when you practice speaking tests such as IELTS, TOEFL.

+/ Not only English, you can also choose to read news in other languages ​​such as Vietnamese, French, German, Italian… To choose a language for reading news on the app Gorgeously, you just need to choose the same language for your phone. For example, if you want to read news on the app Gorgeously in English, change the language on your phone to English; If you want to read news on the app Gorgeously in French, change the language on your phone to French.

+/ In addition, you can choose to read news on the app Gorgeously with dark mode. When you turn on this mode, the phone screen will filter out blue light – a type of light that is harmful to the eyes and the quality of sleep. Therefore, the phenomenon of eyestrain is avoided.

+/ Currently, the good news app Gorgeously is available on the Google Play platform and is coming soon on the iOS platform.

+/ Link to download the Gorgeously good news app: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.everythingsunny.goodnews

🍀Benefits of learning English by reading news

Here are the indispensable benefits of reading news in English every day:

+/ Reading news in English will help you improve your vocabulary a lot. Each article on a specific topic will use a list of vocabulary relating to that topic. Therefore, you will encounter many synonyms and collocations which are very applicable when you want to speak/write about a topic like the topic in the article.

+/ Reading English news will keep you up-to-date, enhance your understanding of science and social knowledge from all fields of life, and give you numerous ideas when you speak/write/argue about one specific topic.

+/ You will have a chance to exercise reading skills by reading news: scanning skill, skimming skill, reading comprehension skill. Those skills are especially practical in some international exams like TOEIC, IELTS or TOEFL.

+/ You will also learn a number of structures and phrases used in the writing style (formal) that you can apply when writing essays (essay).

+/ This is also both a source of entertainment and relaxation with articles illustrated by a bunch of colorful images and vivid videos.

🍀Steps to learn English by reading news

Once you’ve downloaded the app Gorgeously, change the language on your phone to English (if you have not already changed). Thus, there are articles in English on the app Gorgeously. Note: if you use another language on your phone, you will see articles in the corresponding language on the app Gorgeously. 

Steps to read English news effectively:

Step 1: Choose an article with the topic you are interested in

When you first start learning, you should choose an article which is of moderate length and has not too many new words to avoid being discouraged. Choosing the news that you are fond of will help you remember vocabulary and structures more effectively than the topics that you less care about.

Step 2: Read the article preliminarily

In this step, you read the title and subtitles to understand the main content of the article. This is also called the skimming skill which is used in international tests like TOEIC, IELTS and TOEFL.

Step 3: Read the article entirely

Now, start reading the details from the beginning to the end. Maybe you will come across a few or many new words, try to guess their meaning based on the context in the article before looking up the dictionary.

Step 4: Look up the dictionary

Write new words / phrases in the article onto a vocabulary book/ word file or excel file and look up those words. At the same time, you should always remember the word type and look for family words to greatly ameliorate your vocabulary.

Here are some online English-English dictionaries: Oxford, Cambridge, Collins (you can see the definition in English) 

In addition, you should also note down the international phonetics of the words and click on the image of the small speaker next to the words, then listen and practice their pronunciations.

You can even save phrases or sentences that you find interesting and useful for your English language tests. .

Note: To remember well English vocabulary, you need to review those words at the weekend/ end of the month, or you can read that English article again.

Step 5: Summarize the article.

Re-read and summarize the article in 4 – 5 sentences to make sure you understand the main content of the article.

Step 6: Give your personal opinion (Optional)

This is an effective way for you to practice your critical thinking skill. In this step, you say that you agree or disagree with the author and explain why in the comment section below each article. By dint of that, you will have a deeper understanding of the topic and all information in the article.

Note: Besides English, you absolutely can apply the 6 steps above to learn other languages ​​such as French, German… on the app Gorgeously.

So, we have discovered the benefits of reading English news and how to read English news effectively to boost English proficiency. Don’t forget to download the Gorgeously good news app on Google Play to practice your English everyday. Have fun! 💝
