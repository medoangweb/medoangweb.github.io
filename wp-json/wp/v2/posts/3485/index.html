Mastering a language takes time because there’s lot of vocabulary you need to learn, and success is small steps taken day by day. Here in this article, I will share common expressions in French, which I think you will find useful to express exactly what to say in your first conversations with French people.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  1. Bonjour\/ Salut ! – Hello\/ Hi!<\/li>
  2. Bonsoir ! – Good evening! <\/li>
  3. Quoi de neuf ? – What’s up? <\/li>
  4. Comment \u00e7a va ? – How’s it going? <\/li>
  5. Pas grand chose. – Nothing much. <\/li>
  6. Enchant\u00e9(e) ! – Nice to meet you! <\/li>
  7. Ah bon ? – Is that so? <\/li>
  8. Comment \u00e7a ? – How come? <\/li>
  9. Tout \u00e0 fait ! – Definitely! <\/li>
  10. Bien s\u00fbr ! – Of course! <\/li>
  11. Sans fa\u00e7on ! – No way! <\/li>
  12. \u00c7a fait un bail ! – Long time no see! <\/li>
  13. Je l\u2019ai fait ! – I did it! <\/li>
  14. Parle plus fort ! – Speak up! <\/li>
  15. Viens ici. – Come here. <\/li>
  16. Toi d\u2019abord. Apres toi. – Please go first. After you. <\/li>
  17. Merci de me laisser passer. – Thanks for letting me go first. <\/li>
  18. De rien. – No problem. <\/li>
  19. Fais comme j\u2019ai dit. – Do as I say. <\/li>
  20. C\u2019est assez ! – This is the limit! <\/li>
  21. Comme c\u2019est mignon ! – How cute!<\/li>
  22. Ce n\u2019est pas tes oignons ! – None of your business!<\/li>
  23. On se voit quand ? – When are we meeting? <\/li>
  24. \u00c0 bient\u00f4t ! – See you soon! <\/li>
  25. \u00c0 demain ! – See you tomorrow! <\/li>
  26. Bonne journ\u00e9e ! – Have a nice day! <\/li>
  27. Bon weekend ! – Have a nice weekend! <\/li>
  28. Pardon! – Pardon!<\/li>
  29. Ce n\u2019est pas grave ! – It\u2019s not serious! <\/li>
  30. \u00c0 ce soir. – See you tonight.<\/li>
  31. On se verra ce soir. – We will see each other tonight.<\/li>
  32. Pas maintenant. – Not now.<\/li>
  33. La prunelle de mes yeux ! – The apple of my eyes!<\/li>
  34. \u00c7a d\u00e9pend. – It depends.<\/li>
  35. \u00c7a d\u00e9pend de la situation. – It depends on the situation.<\/li>
  36. Je t\u2019aime tant. – I love you so much.<\/li>
  37. Mille mercis\/Merci mille fois ! – Thanks a million!<\/li>
  38. Un grand merci ! – A big thank!<\/li>
  39. Quel conte de f\u00e9e ! – What a fairy tale! <\/li>
  40. Jamais de la vie ! – Never in life!<\/li>
  41. Comme nous sommes heureux ! – How happy we are!<\/li>
  42. Quelle chance nous avons !- How lucky we are!<\/li>
  43. Esp\u00e9rons ! – Let\u2019s hope!<\/li>
  44. Tiens ! – Look at that!<\/li>
  45. Quoi ! – What!<\/li>
  46. Au secours ! – Help!<\/li>
  47. Allons ! – Come on!<\/li>
  48. Vite ! – Quick!<\/li>
  49. Fais des beaux r\u00eaves ! – Have nice dreams!<\/li>
  50. Est-ce que vous savez faire la cuisine ? – Can you cook? <\/li>
  51. Tu me manques. – I miss you.<\/li>
  52. Vietnam me manque. – I miss Vietnam.<\/li>
  53. Je te manque ? – Do you miss me?<\/li>
  54. Prenez soin de vous ! – Take care of yourself!<\/li>
  55. Ne soyez pas en retard. – Don\u2019t be late.<\/li>
  56. J\u2019y pense. – I\u2019m thinking about it.<\/li>
  57. J\u2019en ai besoin. – I need it.<\/li>
  58. Moi aussi. – So do I.<\/li>
  59. Moi non plus. – Neither do I.<\/li>
  60. Fais-le toi-m\u00eame ! – Do it yourself!<\/li>
  61. \u00c7a ne fait rien. – It doesn\u2019t matter..<\/li>
  62. \u00c7a m\u2019est \u00e9gal. – I don\u2019t mind.<\/li>
  63. C\u2019est une bonne id\u00e9e. – It\u2019s a good idea.<\/li>
  64. Tu es en avance. – You are early.<\/li>
  65. Il est dehors. – He is outside.<\/li>
  66. T\u2019as compris\/T\u2019as pig\u00e9 ? – Got it?<\/li>
  67. C\u2019est un cadeau ! – It\u2019s a gift!<\/li>
  68. Asseyez-vous ! – Sit down!<\/li>
  69. Je n\u2019en peux plus. – I can\u2019t take it anymore.<\/li>
  70. C\u2019est \u00e0 prendre ou \u00e0 laisser ! – Take it or leave it!<\/li>
  71. On verra. – We\u2019ll see.<\/li>
  72. Ne quittez pas ! – Hold on the line!<\/li>
  73. Vous pouvez disposer. – You may go.<\/li>
  74. Vous \u00eates enceinte, vous pouvez passer devant. – You are pregnant, you can go next.<\/li>
  75. Qu\u2019est-ce qui ne va pas chez Carla ? – What\u2019s wrong with Carla? <\/li>
  76. Tais-toi!\/Taisez-vous ! – Keep quiet!<\/li>
  77. Ne me fais pas attendre ! – Don\u2019t keep me waiting!<\/li>
  78. Cela ne fait rien. – It does not matter.<\/li>
  79. Ne t\u2019en fais pas\/Ne vous en faites pas\/Ne t\u2019inqui\u00e8te pas ! – Don\u2019t worry<\/li>
  80. Pas de quoi\/Il n\u2019y a pas de quoi. – Don\u2019t mention it.<\/li>
  81. Comment \u00e7a se fait ? – How is that\/How can that be?<\/li>
  82. \u00c0 un de ces jours ! – See you later!<\/li>
  83. Enchant\u00e9(e) de faire votre connaissance. – Nice to meet you\/ Delighted to make your acquaintance.<\/li>
  84. Je suis chez moi. – I\u2019m at home.<\/li>
  85. Peut-\u00eatre une autre fois. – Perhaps another time.<\/li>
  86. Je veux bien. –  I would be happy to\/Gladly.<\/li>
  87. Je plaisantais. – I was joking.<\/li>
  88. Oui, exactement ! – Yes, that\u2019s right!<\/li>
  89. J\u2019y serrai. – I\u2019ll be there.<\/li>
  90. C\u2019est emb\u00eatant !: That\u2019s annoying!<\/li>
  91. O\u00f9 sont les toilettes ? – Where are the (public) toilets?<\/li>
  92. Quelle poisse ! – Just my luck!<\/li>
  93. Veinard(e) !: Lucky you!<\/li>
  94. C\u2019est tordant ! – It\u2019s hilarious!<\/li>
  95. C\u2019est quand ton anniversaire ? – When is your birthday?<\/li>
  96. D\u2019accord ! – Agree!<\/li>
  97. Je suis d\u2019accord. – I agree.<\/li>
  98. Tu es d\u2019accord ? – Do you agree?<\/li>
  99. Bon voyage ! – Have a good trip!<\/li>
  100. Au revoir ! – Goodbye! <\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
