It is true that water is an essential element for all life on earth.  And the word \u2018eau\u2019<\/strong> which means water<\/strong> is also used in many everyday expressions in the French language.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

So, let’s discover the 15 common phrases with l\u2019eau<\/strong> – a feminine noun in this episode! \ud83d\udca6<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1- Comme un poisson dans l’eau <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be very comfortable<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Je me sens comme un poisson dans l’eau dans ma nouvelle \u00e9quipe.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(I feel like a fish in the water on my new team.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2- Clair comme de l’eau de roche<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be clear, obvious<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Ton explication est parfaite. J’ai bien compris, c’est clair comme de l’eau de roche.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Your explanation is perfect. I understood correctly, it is very clear.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3- \u00c0 l\u2019eau de rose <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be sentimental, playing on simple emotions, lacking in vigor<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: C’est un roman \u00e0 l’eau de rose.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(It is a sentimental novel.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3- Tomber \u00e0 l\u2019eau<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be canceled or abandoned, no longer be considered<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Tomber dans le lac, tomber dans le panneau<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: La soir\u00e9e qu’on avait pr\u00e9vue tombe \u00e0 l’eau.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(The party we had planned is canceled.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4- \u00catre sous l\u2019eau <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be overwhelmed, often by an amount of work or tasks to complete<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: \u00catre submerg\u00e9<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Je suis sous l\u2019eau et n’arrive pas \u00e0 faire tout mon travail.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(I am snowed under with work and I cannot do it all.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5- Se jeter \u00e0 l\u2019eau <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to take the plunge, to force yourself to make an important decision and take risks<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: C’est d\u00e9cid\u00e9, je me jette \u00e0 l’eau : demain je lui avoue tout !<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(It’s decided, I take the plunge: tomorrow I’ll confess everything!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

6- Aller \u00e0 vau-l\u2019eau <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be lost, to go out of control<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Vous allez \u00e0 vau-l’eau en n’\u00e9coutant pas mes conseils.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(You are going out of control because of not listening to my advice.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

7- Avoir l\u2019eau \u00e0 la bouche <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to salivate with envy, to be attracted to something that makes you want or curious<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714\ufe0fSynonym: Mettre<\/em><\/strong> <\/em>l\u2019eau \u00e0 la bouche<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Rien que de lire le menu, \u00e7a met d\u00e9j\u00e0 l\u2019eau \u00e0 la bouche !<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Just reading the menu makes your mouth water already!)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

8- Mettre de l\u2019eau dans son vin <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be more moderate<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Briel est plus agr\u00e9able depuis qu’il a mis de l’eau dans son vin.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Briel is more pleasant since he is more moderate.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

9- Se m\u00e9fier de l\u2019eau qui dort<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to beware of calm individuals as they can turn out to be the most dangerous<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: On dit qu’il faut se m\u00e9fier de l’eau qui dort.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Still waters run deep.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

10- Jeter le b\u00e9b\u00e9 avec l’eau du bain<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to lose  sight of the essentials, to separate the good from the bad and throw all things away<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Cependant, il jette le b\u00e9b\u00e9 avec l’eau du bain, et cette attitude est irresponsable.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(However, he separates the good from the bad and throws all things away, and this attitude is irresponsible.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

11- Se ressembler comme deux gouttes d\u2019eau<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be alike like two drops of water, to look alike in a perfectly identical way<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Elles se ressemblent comme deux gouttes d’eau.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(They look alike like two drops of water.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

12- Il y a de l’eau dans le gaz.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: Things are not going very well and the atmosphere is tense.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714\ufe0fExample: J’entends souvent les voisins se disputer. Il y a de l’eau dans le gaz dans leur couple.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(I often hear the neighbors arguing. Things are not going very well and the atmosphere is tense.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

13- De l’eau a coul\u00e9 sous les ponts.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: Time has passed, things have evolved and no longer look like they were before.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714\ufe0fSynonym: De l’eau est pass\u00e9e sous les ponts.<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: L’eau a coul\u00e9 sous les ponts depuis la derni\u00e8re fois que nous nous sommes rencontr\u00e9s.  <\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Things have changed since the last time we met.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

14- C\u2019est une goutte d\u2019eau dans la mer.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

 <\/strong>\u2714Meaning: There is a negligible, insignificant, inconsequential effect.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714\ufe0fSynonym: C\u2019est une goutte d\u2019eau dans l\u2019oc\u00e9an.<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Le gouvernement a pris cette mesure mais c\u2019est une goutte d\u2019eau dans la mer. \u00c7a ne va pas r\u00e9soudre le probl\u00e8me.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(The government has taken this step but there is a negligible effect. It will not solve the problem.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

15- C’est la goutte d’eau qui fait d\u00e9border le vase. <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: Enough is enough. It’s the little thing on top of an already difficult situation.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: La coupe est pleine.<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: J’en ai assez ! L\u00e0, c’est la goutte d’eau qui fait d\u00e9border le vase !<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(It\u2019s enough for me. Enough is enough.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n
