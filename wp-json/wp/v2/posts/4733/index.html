Hello everyone, there are many French idioms with parts of the body. Here are some common expressions related to body parts, their definitions and examples. Let\u2019s discover right away!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#1 – Avoir le bras long <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to have a lot of influence<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Rio a le bras long, le pr\u00e9sident lui-m\u00eame l’\u00e9coute.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Rio has influence, the president listens to him.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#2 – Avoir les yeux plus gros que le ventre <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: not to be able to eat everything you have taken, to want too much for your abilities<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Tu n’as fini aucun de tes plats, comme d’habitude, tu as eu les yeux plus gros que le ventre.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(You haven’t finished any of your meals, as usual, your eyes were bigger than your stomach.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#3 – En avoir plein le dos <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be fed up with <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: En avoir plein le casque<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: J’en ai plein le dos du chantage.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(I\u2019m fed up with blackmail.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#4- Les doigts dans le nez <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: very easily, without difficulty<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Haut la main<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Il va gagner les doigts dans le nez.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(He will win without difficulty.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#5 – Avoir le nez creux <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to have flair, to have intuition, to predict an event correctly<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Avoir du nez, avoir le nez fin<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: D’habitude j’ai le nez creux pour ce genre de trucs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Usually I have intuition for this kind of stuff.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#6 – Attraper la grosse t\u00eate <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be arrogant, to be conceited, to become pretentious<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Avoir la grosse t\u00eate, avoir la gueule de bois<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Je pense qu’ils ont la grosse t\u00eate, vous comprenez ?<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(I think they are arrogant, you know?)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#7 – Faire la fine bouche <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be fussy, to be demanding, to be choosy<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Mon ch\u00e9ri, parfois il ne faut pas faire la fine bouche.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(My darling, sometimes you shouldn’t be choosy.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#8 – Avoir les \u00e9paules larges <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be able to endure a lot of tasks <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Avoir le dos large <\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Allez-y, j’ai les \u00e9paules larges.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Let\u2019s go! I’m able to do a lot of things.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#9 – Rentre par une oreille et sortir par l\u2019autre<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to go in one ear and out the other<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Vous savez dans notre m\u00e9tier, ces choses-l\u00e0, \u00e7a entre par une oreille et \u00e7a sort par l\u2019autre.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(You know in our business, these things go in one ear and go out the other.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#10 – Par-dessus la jambe <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: carelessly<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714\ufe0fExample: Dans beaucoup d’\u00e9coles, les enfants estiment que le sujet est trait\u00e9 par-dessus la jambe.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(In many schools, children think that the topic is treated carelessly.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#11 – Avoir l’estomac dans les talons <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to be very hungry <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Apr\u00e8s quatre heures de marche, les randonneurs avaient l’estomac dans les talons.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(After four hours of walking, the hikers were very hungry.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#12 – Manger sur le pouce<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to eat quickly<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Comme on avait peur de manquer le train de 12h30, on a mang\u00e9 sur le pouce et on a couru \u00e0 la gare.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(As we were afraid of missing the 12:30 pm train, we ate quickly and ran to the station.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#13 – Dormir sur ses deux oreilles <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to sleep soundly, to sleep quietly<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Malgr\u00e9 le bruit que faisaient les voisins pour l’anniversaire de leur fille, j’ai dormi sur mes deux oreilles.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Despite the noise the neighbors were making on their daughter’s birthday, I slept soundly.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#14 –  Avoir la main verte <\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: have a green thumb, to have green fingers, to know how to care for plants, to be good at gardening<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Synonym: Avoir le pouce vert <\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Elisa a toujours aim\u00e9 la campagne, les fleurs, les plantes vertes. D’ailleurs, comme disent les jardiniers, elle a la main verte.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Elisa has always loved the countryside, flowers, green plants.  Besides, as gardeners say, she has green fingers.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

#15 – Avoir la bouche en cul de poule<\/strong> <\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Meaning: to have a small pursed mouth, to be honeyed, to be sugary<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u2714Example: Miley a la bouche en cul de poule. Gr\u00e2ce \u00e0 \u00e7a, sa maman lui ach\u00e8te tout ce qu’elle veut. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

(Miley has a small pursed mouth.  Thanks to that, her mom buys her whatever she wants.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n
